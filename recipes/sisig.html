“Sizzling Pork Sisig: the rockstar of Filipino food — loud, sizzling, and guaranteed to steal the show. It’s crispy, chewy, tangy, and spicy all at once, like your ex who couldn’t decide what they wanted. Served on a hot plate that crackles louder than chismis sa kanto, topped with egg and calamansi, this dish isn’t just food — it’s a performance.”

Ingredients:

  • Maskara ni Peppa pig ung may tenga
  • Salt and peppa
  • Atay ng manok ng kapitbahay nyo
  • Seboyas sabi ni Jiphre na Manileño
  • Camalansi or Lemon
  • Sili kung gusto mo maanghang
  • Toyo ng jowa mo

Step by Step:

  1. Season pork belly slices with salt and pepper. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Grill pork belly and chicken liver until well done. Pork skin should be crispy.
  3. Once grilled, chop the pork belly and chicken liver into small fine pieces.
  4. In a bowl, combine the chopped meat, onions, lemon juice and chili pepper. Season with soy sauce and pepper.
  5. Warm it in a pan with a bit of oil if desired. Alternatively, heat a sizzling plate and add a some butter or margarine. Add the Sisig and serve while still sizzling hote.
